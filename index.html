FAQs

Why do marketing & product teams switch to Netmera?

They're tired of waiting on developers to tag events, connect data sources, or fix broken integrations. Campaigns take weeks to launch because every step requires IT approval. Netmera removes that dependency. Tagless Data Capture tracks user behavior automatically. Customer data from apps, websites, and CRM systems syncs into unified profiles without custom code. Teams build segments, design journeys, and launch campaigns across all channels themselves.

How quickly can we get started?

Initial setup and integration typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on your user base, the products you choose, and the number of events to track. Once integrated, your team can launch their first campaign within hours using the no-code interface.

Do we need developers to use Netmera?

Minimal developer work during initial setup and integration. After that, your team handles everything: building campaigns, tracking events, creating segments, launching journeys. The drag-and-drop Journey builder and messaging tools remove the technical bottleneck. No SQL required. No constant IT requests.

Which channels does Netmera support?

Push notifications (mobile and web), email, SMS, WhatsApp, in-app messages, pop-ups, surveys, geofencing, and Live Activities. Teams can run campaigns on individual channels or orchestrate them across multiple touchpoints using the Journey Builder.

Is Netmera secure enough for regulated industries?

Banks and telecoms trust Netmera with their customer data. We hold ISO 20000, ISO 27017, ISO/IEC 27701, and ISO 22301. GDPR compliant. Independently tested with regular penetration assessments. For Turkish companies, KVKK, IYS and BDKK compliance is built in.

Can Netmera handle our data volume as we scale?

We process 30 billion events monthly from 500+ million users. The platform delivers 30+ billion notifications each month and handles 200TB+ of real-time data without performance issues. Brands scale from millions to billions of events without platform changes.

How does Netmera unify customer data from different sources?

Netmera's SDK integrates with your mobile app (iOS, Android, Flutter, React Native, Cordova). For the web, the Tag Manager captures behavioral data automatically. Backend integrations connect via REST API or FTP to pull data from your CRM, data warehouse, or third-party tools like Adjust. All of it flows into one unified customer profile without custom code.

What support do we get during and after implementation?

You're assigned a dedicated Customer Onboarding Manager and Solution Architect from day one. They handle initial SDK integration, channel setup, and implementation check-ins. Once live, your dedicated Customer Success Manager takes over with ongoing strategic support. You also get access to 7×24 professional support along with comprehensive user and developer guides.

How is Netmera priced?

Pricing is based on monthly active users (MAU). Contact us for a custom quote based on your user base and the products you need.

What kind of results do companies see with Netmera?

TOD saw purchases increase 187–190% among users who enabled push notifications. N Kolay doubled premium membership completion rates from 4.17% to 9.16% with automated behavioral triggers. DenizBank achieved a 10x increase in click-through rates for loan applications. Mackolik sends 20K+ notifications daily with 20% retention improvement.
